About Me

I am a researcher in the Computer Security Department at NEC Labs America in Princeton, NJ. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Purdue University. My advisors were Prof. Dongyan Xu and Prof. Xiangyu Zhang. I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Utah, where I was working in the Flux Research Group. Prof. John Regehr and Prof. Eric Eide co-advised my Master's research. I did my undergraduate study in Computer Science at Sunmoon University in Korea.
